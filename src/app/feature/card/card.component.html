.ea-card

The ea-card is built to hold a header, content, and footer labeled as .ea-card-header, .ea-card-content, and .ea-card-footer respectively.

The `.ea-card` class is designed to hold the three objects The `.ea-card-content` is intended to hold what is in the card that is not the header or the footer.

.ea-card-header

The `.ea-card-header` holds the title of the card and can optionally hold a button that toggles an `ea-card-menu`. The header can either be aligned with flexbox or grid depending on what attribute you choose for it.

.ea-card-content

The `.ea-card-content` is meant to hold the content of the card. If there is no header and no footer, it would be the only child of the `ea.card`, which would many it a potentially unecessary child element. We planned for having a header and or a footer more often then not on average.

The `.ea-card-content` is meant to hold the content of the card. If there is no header and no footer, it would be the only child of the `ea.card`, which would many it a potentially unecessary child element.
